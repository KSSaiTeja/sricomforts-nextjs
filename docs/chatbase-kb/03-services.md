# Sri Comforts — Services

Sri Comforts offers three core service journeys on the website: How We Work, AMC Plans, and Service Request.

---

## 1) How We Work (/services/how-we-work)

End-to-end HVAC process from first site survey through commissioning and AMC.

Positioning:
- Unified process where design, installation, and service teams work as one
- Load calculation and AutoCAD layout first — then precision install, commissioning, and proactive AMC
- Built for South India’s climate and multi-city portfolios

Process stages:
1. **Survey & assessment** — heat load calculations, site surveys, stakeholder workshops before specifying units
2. **System design** — VRF/split specifications, duct layouts, electrical load plans for real occupancy and peak demand
3. **Modular / phased delivery** — work phased around occupancy; coordinating risers, plant rooms, and tenant fit-outs
4. **Installation** — factory-trained teams for ducting, piping, and electrical with minimal disruption
5. **Commissioning** — airflow verification, refrigerant charge checks, performance sign-off before handover
6. **Ongoing support / AMC** — unified service record across units, visits, and parts
7. **Lifecycle services** — retrofit planning, energy audits, expansion readiness, refrigerant compliance guidance

Portfolio visibility:
- Single point of contact across Hyderabad, Bengaluru, and Chennai (and broader South India footprint)
- Consolidated reporting, AMC schedules, and service history for multi-site clients

Value of the process:
- Maximum system reliability via verified commissioning and proactive AMC
- Scalable operation with one service partner across South India
- Affordable / rapid ROI through right-sized systems and energy-efficient design

## 2) AMC Plans (/services/amc)

Annual Maintenance Contracts for HVAC systems — proactive maintenance instead of reactive breakdowns.

Who it is for:
- Single-site residential splits through multi-building commercial VRF networks
- Facilities that need uptime, predictable cost, and documented service history

What AMC typically includes (as described on the site):
- **Preventive maintenance** — quarterly / seasonal inspections (filters, coils, refrigerant levels, electrical)
- **Priority response** — priority dispatch for AMC clients (Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai called out)
- **Genuine / authorized parts** — sourced through authorized channels to protect warranty and compatibility
- **Service records** — digital logs of units, visits, and parts replaced
- **Energy optimization** — coil cleaning, airflow balancing, control calibration during visits
- **Flexible plan tiers** — scaled by unit count, criticality, and response needs
- **Compliance support** — refrigerant handling documentation, F-gas guidance, audit-ready records

AMC operating cycle:
1. Systematic preventive inspections with findings shared to facility teams
2. Priority emergency / breakdown response with equipment history available to technicians
3. Authorized repairs and post-repair verification
4. Reporting: service summaries, energy notes, upgrade recommendations

AMC value:
- Maximum uptime / fewer tenant complaints
- Predictable costs vs surprise repair bills
- Extended equipment life

## 3) Service Request (/services/service-request)

For emergency breakdowns, scheduled maintenance visits, and performance concerns.

How requests are handled:
1. **Easy intake** — website, service desk call, or AMC account manager
2. **Smart routing** — by location, equipment type, and urgency
3. **Transparent ETA** — AMC clients get priority ETAs; all callers receive confirmation/updates
4. **On-site diagnosis** — root-cause checks (controls, airflow, refrigerant, electrical)
5. **Genuine repairs** — authorized parts and manufacturer procedures
6. **Follow-up** — documented outcomes for compliance and planning
7. **Emergency coverage** — escalated handling for critical spaces (e.g. data centers, hospitals, production floors)

Request types:
- **Breakdown** — emergency cooling restoration
- **Maintenance** — scheduled AMC or ad-hoc visits
- **Performance** — uneven cooling, rising bills, noise complaints
- **Follow-up documentation** — closed-loop service summaries

Service value:
- Fast response via dedicated routing and priority AMC dispatch
- Strong first-time fix focus with factory-trained technicians and genuine parts
- Complete request visibility from intake to closure
