# Sri Comforts — Company Overview

## Who we are

Sri Comforts (Sri Comfort Air Products & Services) is a South India HVAC company delivering design, installation, commissioning, and service for commercial, industrial, healthcare, hospitality, IT, and residential spaces.

Key facts:
- Founded / operating since: 2001
- Experience: 25+ years of HVAC excellence across South India
- Footprint: 8 cities with local teams and shared standards
- Team: 230+ engineers and technicians
- Promise: 24-hour service response on complaints and queries
- Delivery model: design-first, service-backed — one partner from survey through AMC

## What we deliver

End-to-end HVAC & R solutions, including:
- VRV / VRF systems
- Central air conditioning
- Ductable systems and AHUs
- Clean rooms and cold rooms
- Basement / industrial ventilation
- Precision cooling for IT and critical spaces
- Installation, commissioning, and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

## How we work (summary)

1. Site survey and heat-load assessment
2. System design (including AutoCAD / engineering layouts)
3. Precision installation with phased delivery where buildings are occupied
4. Commissioning and performance verification
5. Ongoing AMC and service support

## Brand partnerships

Sri Comforts works with leading HVAC brands and brings design, install, and service execution, including:
- Daikin India — authorized top-tier / authorized dealer relationship (VRV, central air, ductable systems, AHUs)
- O General — authorized partner
- Also referenced portfolio brands: LG and Panasonic

The product brand matters; delivery and accountability are Sri Comforts’.

## Values

- Design-first delivery — drawings and site-fit planning before install
- Service without pause — 24-hour response on complaints and queries
- Integrated systems — VRV, central air, clean rooms, cold rooms, ventilation working together
- Partnership depth — authorized brands with end-to-end accountability
