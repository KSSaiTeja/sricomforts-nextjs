## Services & AMC

**What is AMC?**  
Annual Maintenance Contract — scheduled preventive maintenance, priority breakdown response, genuine/authorized parts, and documented service history so systems stay efficient year after year.

**What does AMC include?**  
Typical coverage described on the site: preventive inspections, priority response, genuine parts, digital service records, energy optimization during visits, flexible tiers, and compliance/documentation support.

**Do you only maintain systems you installed?**  
The site positions AMC for installed portfolios and multi-site networks; for whether your existing make/model can be covered, contact the team for confirmation.

**How fast do you respond to breakdowns?**  
Company promise: 24-hour response on complaints and queries. AMC clients get priority dispatch / priority ETAs. Critical spaces (hospitals, data centers, production) can be escalated.

**Can you handle multi-site portfolios?**  
Yes. Sri Comforts emphasizes single-partner visibility across sites (including Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai) with consolidated reporting and AMC schedules.
