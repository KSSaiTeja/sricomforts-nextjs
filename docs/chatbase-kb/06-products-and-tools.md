# Sri Comforts — Products, Systems & Website Tools

## Cooling systems commonly discussed

Sri Comforts delivers and supports systems including:
- **VRV / VRF** — multi-zone commercial and premium residential
- **Central air conditioning** — larger buildings and campuses
- **Ductable systems** — ducted comfort cooling
- **AHUs (Air Handling Units)** — central plant air distribution
- **Precision AC** — IT / data / server environments
- **Clean room HVAC** — regulated / controlled environments
- **Cold rooms** — refrigerated storage and process areas
- **Ventilation** — basement, industrial exhaust / makeup air, kitchen ventilation
- **Chillers** — referenced in major project deliveries (government, healthcare, IT, hospitality)

Exact product selection depends on heat load, zoning, redundancy, and budget. Sri Comforts designs the system; brand mix may include Daikin, O General, LG, Panasonic depending on the project.

## Design & delivery capabilities

- Heat load analysis and right-sizing (avoid overspend on capacity)
- AutoCAD / MEP engineering drawings
- Occupied-building phased installation
- Commissioning: airflow balance, refrigerant verification, performance sign-off
- Post-sales AMC with digital service history
- Energy audits, retrofit planning, expansion readiness

## Free tool: AC Tonnage Calculator

URL: /resources/ac-tonnage-calculator

Purpose:
- Helps homeowners and light commercial visitors estimate cooling capacity needs from room inputs (size, occupancy-related factors, sun exposure, etc.).

Important limitations:
- It is an estimation tool, not a final engineering load calculation.
- Wrong tonnage can cause short cycling, humidity issues, high bills, or insufficient cooling.
- For villas, multi-room homes, offices, or VRF projects, recommend a Sri Comforts consultation / site assessment.

Related education:
- Blog article on the site about sizing AC tonnage without guessing: search blog for “How to size AC tonnage for your space”.
