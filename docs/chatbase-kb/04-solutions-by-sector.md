# Sri Comforts — Solutions by Sector

Sri Comforts provides sector-specific HVAC solutions across South India. Each solution combines design, installation, commissioning, and AMC.

---

## Commercial HVAC (/solutions/commercial)

For offices, retail parks, commercial campuses, and multi-tenant buildings.

Challenges addressed:
- Multi-zone complexity and hot/cold spots
- Peak load variability through the day
- Diverse facility types needing tailored ducting / VRF / maintenance
- High uptime expectations from tenants and facility managers

Capabilities:
- VRF system design (AutoCAD layouts, occupancy- and budget-aware)
- Precision installation with phased work in occupied buildings
- AMC with scheduled inspections and rapid response
- Ducting and airflow optimization
- Lifecycle support (warranty management, retrofits)

Example use cases:
- Office tower retrofit (split → centralized VRF)
- Multi-tenant campus standardization of AMC
- Energy audit and upgrade
- New commercial fit-out (design to commissioning)

## IT Services HVAC (/solutions/it-services)

For data centers, server rooms, NOCs, and tech campuses.

Challenges:
- High heat density at rack level
- 24/7 uptime / failover needs
- Humidity and filtration control for electronics
- Rapid scaling as racks are added

Capabilities:
- Precision AC (in-row / perimeter) sized to rack layout and redundancy
- N+1 / redundant design so maintenance does not mean downtime
- Monitoring and priority AMC for IT clients
- Hot/cold aisle planning and containment-aware airflow
- Expansion-ready modular designs

Example use cases:
- Server room build-out
- Data hall retrofit from comfort cooling to precision cooling
- NOC / operations center comfort + adjacent IT loads
- Tech campus AMC standardization

## Industrial & Pharma (/solutions/industrial-pharma)

For clean rooms, cold storage, regulated plants, and manufacturing floors.

Challenges:
- Clean room particle / pressure / HEPA requirements
- Cold room / process temperature control
- Validation documentation for audits
- Industrial heat loads and ventilation

Capabilities:
- Clean room HVAC aligned to classification with documented commissioning support
- Cold room refrigeration with preventive maintenance

- Audit-ready AMC (logs, spare-parts traceability)
- Phased retrofit and expansion while production continues

Example use cases:
- API manufacturing zones
- Warehouse cold chain
- Packaging clean rooms
- Heavy industry production halls

Capabilities:
- OT and critical-zone HVAC
- Isolation and ICU pressure-room support
- Redundant / priority AMC for hospitals

- Multi-specialty hospital campus HVAC + centralized AMC
- Diagnostic labs

- Renovation in occupied healthcare buildings

## Residential (/solutions/residential)

For homes, villas, duplexes, and apartments.

Challenges:
- Wrong-sized units (short cycling, humidity, high bills)
- Poor installation quality
- Rising energy costs on older units
- Difficulty finding authorized, trustworthy technicians

Capabilities:
- Tonnage / room-by-room assessment (free sizing consultation messaging on site)
- Authorized installation following manufacturer standards
- VRF for large homes / multi-zone villas
- AMC and seasonal pre-summer service (24hr response messaging on AMC)
- Honest repair-vs-replace guidance

Example use cases:
- New villa / whole-home VRF with architect coordination
- Apartment upgrade to efficient inverter units
- Dedicated home-office zones
- Pre-summer tune-up
