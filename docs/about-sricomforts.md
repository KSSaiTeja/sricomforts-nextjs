Sri Comforts (Sri Comfort Air Products & Services) is a premier HVAC&R solutions provider in South India, headquartered in Secunderabad. Since 2001, they have grown into an authorized top-tier dealer for Daikin, specializing in advanced commercial, industrial, and high-end residential cooling. [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]  
They operate across eight cities in South India with a strong team of over 230 engineers and technicians. [1]  
Key details about the company include: 

• Core Services: Evaluation, design, and installation of HVAC systems (VRV, central air conditioning, ductable systems, and AHUs), clean room/cold room solutions, and basement/staircase ventilation. 
• Specialization: They are widely known as pioneers in VRV (Variable Refrigerant Volume) systems in the region. They carry major brands like Daikin, O General, LG, and Panasonic. 
• Brand Promise: A guaranteed response and service within 24 hours for complaints and queries. 
• Clientele: IT parks, real estate developments, large educational institutions, hospitals (e.g., Omega Hospitals), manufacturing plants, pharma industries, and luxury residential villas. 
• Corporate Structure: They boast specialized, segregated teams that handle AutoCAD designs, project installation, HR, and post-sales service. 
• Online Store: They run an Online Store offering home air conditioners and maintenance contracts (AMCs). [13, 14, 15]
