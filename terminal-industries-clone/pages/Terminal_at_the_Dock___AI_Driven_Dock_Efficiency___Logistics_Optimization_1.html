Terminal at the Dock

Streamline Trailer Loading and Unloading

The Problem

Is that a dock or a parking lot?

Dock Door Bottlenecks

Trucks waiting for an available door due to misalignment between yard activity and warehouse operations.

Uncoordinated Labor & Equipment

Warehouse staff not ready when trailers are at doors, causing wasted time and increased detention fees.

Load/Unload Delays

Slow processes due to poor dock scheduling, manual documentation, or inadequate equipment.

Cross-Team Communication Gaps

Lack of synchronization between yard jockeys, dock workers, and warehouse managers creates inefficiency..

The Solution

Terminal Solves Problems At the Dock, Differently

01AI-Orchestration, Not Simple Scheduling

Most dock tools only handle scheduling or door assignments. Terminal acts as a true AI orchestration engine, managing moves in real time and dispatching spotters to the right doors at the right moment. Trucks aren’t just scheduled — they keep moving.

02Seamless WMS Integration, Not Siloed Systems

Competitors force operators to run dock activity in a standalone tool that may not talk to warehouse systems. Terminal integrates natively with leading WMS platforms, synchronizing dock schedules with real-time yard activity so the yard never bottlenecks the warehouse.

03Unified Data Layer, Not Fragmented Visibility

Traditional systems rely on static schedules and manual updates, leaving warehouse teams reactive and blind to changes. Terminal creates a unified, real-time data layer that gives receiving teams pre-arrival information so labor, space, and equipment are ready before the trailer arrives.

04AI-Powered Vision, Not Manual Checks

Other platforms depend on barcodes, spreadsheets, or disconnected data feeds. Terminal applies AI-powered computer vision to capture asset IDs, monitor dock door availability, and track movements automatically. That automation eliminates errors and keeps a live, accurate inventory that manual systems cannot match.
