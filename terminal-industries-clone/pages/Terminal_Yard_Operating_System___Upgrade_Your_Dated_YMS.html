What Is Terminal YOS™

Real command of your yard requires transformational technology

01Cutting-Edge Tech Stack

Designed with modern tech from the ground up. Starting at the gate, automate all of your critical yard workflows with AI computer vision, cloud-based infrastructure, and next-gen fixed or mobile cameras.

02SmartYard™ YMS

Reimagine what a YMS could be. SmartYard™ is a seamless, end-to-end YMS that automates your yard workflows from gate check-in and dock appointment scheduling, to trailer check-out and everything in between. Highly configurable. Maximum asset visibility.

03Advanced Yard

Terminal YOS™ goes deeper where other YMS solutions fall short, including: ISR compliance, augmented site security, damage detection and, over time, true agentic AI-powered workflows.

04Yard Networks

Terminal YOS™ was built to scale to all of the yards in your network. This means deep TMS/WMS integrations; a modular platform that can be tuned to each yard; customizable analytics; and single pane of glass visibility to each yard, and all yards, through the Terminal platform.
