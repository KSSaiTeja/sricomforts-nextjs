About us

The yard is the most underestimated control point in supply chain. Terminal Industries is changing that. We’ve built an AI-native Yard Operating System that turns disconnected yard workflows into a real execution layer: visibility, decisions, and action, stitched together in one system. If you’re done with “digital transformation theater” and want the practical future of yard automation, YMS, and AI logistics… hit Follow. We post what’s working, what’s not, and what’s next.

    Bad gate data doesn't stay at the gate. Sometimes it's a wrong trailer number at entry that becomes a mismatched shipment at the dock, or a missed missed BOL scan that becomes a disputed claim weeks later. By the time anyone catches it, the truck is gone. One operator running manual gate logs was seeing a 62% error rate on gate transactions. Every mistake required someone to go back, find the record, and correct it. Terminal's Gate Acceleration and Mobile Capture changed that. Asset data logged automatically. DRL and BOL captured on mobile. Operators stopped double-checking every move because the system gave them something they could actually trust. 62% reduction in gate errors. The data was right the first time.
